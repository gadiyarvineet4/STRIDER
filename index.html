Why Strider exists

"A man can be surrounded by people and still be completely alone."

In 2023 the U.S. Surgeon General declared loneliness a public health epidemic. 1 in 3 men report having no close friends. The number is rising.

Every solution built for this has failed. Because they treated loneliness as the problem. It isn't. Loneliness is what happens when a man loses his sense of who he is and what he is for.

The Foundation

The Code

I
You are not broken. You are between.
Where you are right now is not a verdict. It is a position. Positions change. Men who accept this move forward. Men who don't, stay.
II
Solitude is not failure. It is the forge.
Being alone is not the problem. Fearing it is. The man who is comfortable in his own company connects from wholeness, not need.
III
Identity is built through action, not reflection.
You do not think your way to becoming someone. You act. You show up. You do the thing, especially when you don't feel like it. That is who you are.
IV
A brother makes you accountable to more than yourself.
One man who shows up for you, and for whom you show up, is worth more than a thousand followers. Brotherhood is not sentiment. It is presence.
